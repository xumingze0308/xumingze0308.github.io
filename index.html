Biography

Hi, I'm Mingze. I'm an Applied Scientist at Amazon Rekognition in Seattle.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Indiana University, advised by Prof. David Crandall in 2020. I was a visiting student researcher in Prof. Dhruv Batra's group at Georgia Institute of Technology in 2018. Before that, I received my Master's degree in Computer Science from Indiana University in 2014 and my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Jilin University in 2012.

My research interests lie primarily in the area of computer vision and deep learning. In particular, my recent and ongoing projects consider following applications:

  • Action and activity recognition

  • First-person (egocentric) vision

  • Object tracking and (re-)identification

  • Image and video segmentation

  • Face recognition
